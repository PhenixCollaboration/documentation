NOTE: the data tables are updated on 12/07/2006. They are the same one as in the final figure in the paper published in PRL.

In comparison with the original original data table (version 1) there are two very small changes.
1) In low pT, the systematic errors are reduced slightly after error propagation is done more properly.
2) At high pT, we now take into account the pT dependence of the probability of internal conversion of direct photons. This increases the cocktail by a very small amount in high pT. The change in heavy-flavor electron cross section is less than a percent for most of the momentum range.
